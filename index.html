Our Mission

Our organization pursues several goals that can be identified as our mission.

Care & Protection

Delivering the light of education at the grassroots level and ensuring the rights of the helpless.

Weather Disaster Action

To carry out tree planting program every year to ensure climate protection. Ensuring food, medicine and all assistance to the people of the disaster affected areas.

Saving Children and Woman

Raise public awareness and protest against violence against women and children. The only way to prevent child marriage is with the help of law. Raising voice to prevent eveteasing and preventing women's helplessness.

Our Vision

Team Comrades envisions to improve the lifestyles of disadvantaged communities through access to human rights, health service, legal protection, education, choice of living, livelihood opportunities, and other social supports to ensure non-discrimination, gender equality, social justice and peace.

Who We Are?

Team Comrades is a youth-focused right and development organization having non-profit, non-government and non-political natures, started life on 24 December, 2020 with the initiation of a group of students, dedicated social workers with an aim to promote socio-economic and social status and living standards of the poor and vulnerable communities, especially youth, women and children through their capacity building, awareness-raising, group formation and intervening with felt need-based problem-solving programs by adopting innovative ideas and strategic communication. Team Comrades believes in non-directive, bottom-up, participatory and integrated development frameworks and acts as a catalyst with its beneficiaries.
